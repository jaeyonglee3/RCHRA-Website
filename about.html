About Us

Our vision

What we do

Rotman Commerce Human Resources Association (RCHRA) is a student-run organization that is dedicated to help Rotman Commerce students to explore their interests in the HR industry. We provide opportunities for students to network with HR professionals and learn about Human Resources.

Our Values

Below are our collective values that we hold to the utmost importance

Respect

We recognize the importance of establishing a positive and supportive culture to foster teamwork and collaboration. We model respectful behavior, encourage open communivation, provide opportunities for growth and development, and value diversity.

Boldness

We recognize the importance of taking risks and making bold decisions in order to acheive success. We encourage each other to think outside of the box, take initiative, and propose innovative ideas and solutions.

Diversity

We recognize the importance of creating an environment that is inclusive and welcoming to everyone, regardless of age, race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, religion, disability, or any other characteristic.

Inclusion

We recognize the importance of creating an atmosphere where everyone feels valued, respected, and able to fully participate and contribute.
